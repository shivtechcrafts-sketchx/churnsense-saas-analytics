# SaaS Churn Insights

1. Basic plan users have highest churn.
2. Users inactive for more than 20 days have 70% churn probability.
3. India region shows higher churn compared to USA.
4. Monthly churn increased after price hike.

Business Recommendation:
- Improve onboarding for Basic plan users.
- Send reminders to inactive users.
- Offer discounts to high-risk customers.
